Welcome!

This website will be dedicated to documenting and archiving my progress in my various hobbies and interests.

Through documenting it in the style of old blogs from the late 2000s - early 2010s with some modern design sensibilities, I'm hoping the nostalgia I'm trying to invoke this way will help with motivation and holding myself accountable to making consistent progress.

This is also meant to be a place outside of my social media pages to post my creations and progress without external input, so this can be a casual, low-stakes place where external validation won't affect my motivation.

Pages may be added as I gain new interests depending on how much I'm committed to sticking with it and how much it'll clutter the layout.
